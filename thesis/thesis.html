Intro

Chapter 1

"Imperative"

Tech and computer science have shaped a lot of our perception about design thinking and even often go hand in hand when talking about lucrative industries. It goes far beyond just design thinking. It has shaped plurality, if not majority of our modern analytic approaches. Large portion of applied physics has been transformed into "statistical mehcanis", mathematical theorems and lemmas are being proven through extensive numeric calculations. Often causation links could be discvered just through automated massive data regressions.

It is impossible to overestimate how much our learning process is tied to computation. It is even more prominent in 'mainstream' design professions. Captions like Graphic design, UX design and UI design go, practically always, in unison with 'applications', 'web pages', digital print, and where last one is clearly physical, more and more it lately becomes an auxiliary mean to a digital service behind it.
