Chapter 1

We get to see the several people who constitute the heart of the clan. They share a tea and show the power dynamic going from the top two persons down to other 3, and specifically 1 among them, who will then show us role of the rest in distributing the will of the leader.

SCENE: (INT.) CLAN'S RECEPTION ROOM

CUT FROM BLACK:

The room is a square hall. Quite spacious and generously lit with paper lanterns. It's almost evening now so paper windows are not giving off any light, and the hall feels like inside of a brick, due to red-ish tint of the wood of which it is almost entirely made.

From outside voices and commotion can be heard. Nothing violent, merely an evening more busy than usual.

Main interior doors to the hall open and in walks a delegation of samurai warriors and a handful of servants. Samurais are covered in dirt, grime and occasional splatters of blood. Servants are flocking around them trying to find anything they could tend to. The one - oldest of the servants is standing next to most elaborately dressed of the samurai. He stands there, unlike other servants without budging an eye, same can be said of the 'general'-ish warrior.

[the Leader]

[Second in command], how much did you count?

[Second in command]

Captains relayed around a third lost all across. Precise amount is yet to be counted.

[the Leader] walks across the room to the windows, and slams one open. Several of the paper sheets slide one of them pops out of frame falling.

CHUNG! CHUNG! CHUNG! CHUNG! CHUNG!

SMACK!

We see the location - which is a castle-town amidst foresty mountain slopes. We cut to soldier accomodating themselves, and as much as possible, trying to fit behind the castle walls.
